Santa Monica Pier

The Santa Monica Pier is a must-see place in Southern California. While being a huge tourist attraction out on the water, the Pier has become one of the most prosperous and is considered the heart of Santa Monica. The Pier was built in 1909 and is still standing even after so much construction and renevation. Many amusement park rides such as the famous West Coaster abd the Pacific Wheel can be found here. Lots pof food stands are located here as well with your typical amuesment park food as well as some southern favorites.

Santa Monica Beach

Right next to the Pier is the Santa Monica Beach which is one of the most famous becahes in Southern California. The Beach us about three-and-a-half miles long and it has access to many hotels, shops, and resturants that are located along the coast. Unfortunatly, the Santa Monica Beach is not a dog beach but they are allowed on the boardwalk next door.

Downtown Santa Monica

Near the Pier and Beach is Downtown Santa Monica which has many businuesses for any type of necesity. Many of these are owned by small businuess owners who take pride in all of the work they produce. Downtown Santa Monica also has many events that happen ther such as theur outdoor movie nights and their Christmas tree lighting ceremony.
